BCIS 445/543 DATABASE DESIGN AND IMPLEMENTATION

This course covers the database design process: conceptual design, logical design, and implementation. The course teaches students database design principles and techniques such as E.R.M. and normalization. Oracle9i is used to demonstrate database implementation. Great emphasis is placed on using triggers to enforce business rules during implementation.
