IS 340 – Project #4 Website Design
Developing a Personal or Business Website
Spring 2014

Create a personal or business website using any web design software. Adobe Dreamweaver is preferred. Please make sure that you give a professional appearance to your pages and make a fictitious title for your website.  Use your creativity, experience, and any resources you can find to design an attractive website you will be proud of.

Helpful suggestions for B+ or A grade:

§  Use a template

§ Compact design & professional look

§  Use headings and sub-headings to separate sections of text

§  Use white space wisely - Make sure you have no more than 12 words per line

§  Write concisely - not an essay

§  Highlight important words

§  Use a pale background with dark text or a dark background with light text. Avoid colors which clash, especially combinations which are hard for color-blind people to read.
