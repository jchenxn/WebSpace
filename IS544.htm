IS 544 Information Technology Foundations
This course is designed to provide pre-MBA students foundation knowledge in information technology. Students will learn the strategic and administrative roles of information systems in business, understand how information systems enable businesses to gain a competitive advantage, and anticipate the role of emerging technologies on business processes.
Course objectives: (1). Describe the roles of Information Systems (2). Discuss how Information Systems are used for competitive advantage. (3). Explain how different information systems collect, retrieve, process, store, and disseminate information to the correct organizational units in a timely manner. (4). Discuss how information systems are developed.
