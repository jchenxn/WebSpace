DR. JIM Q. CHEN, PhD

Jim Q. Chen is Professor of Information Systems in the Herberger Business School at St. Cloud State University.

He teaches primarily Database Design & Implementation and Database Security & Auditing. He has also taught Decision Support Systems, Introduction to Computer Information Systems, and advanced programming courses at both undergraduate and graduate levels.

He has authored over 60 articles and instructional materials. His research, primarily involving information ethics, information assurance, web application development methods, knowledge management, and decision support systems, has appeared in such journals as Asia Case Research Journal, Communications of the ACM, Decision Support Systems, Information Systems Management, Systems Development Management, Logistics Information Management, Journal of Internet Commerce, and Journal of Computer Information Systems among others.

Professor Chen is an Editorial Board Member for Journal of Internet Commerce and International Journal of Information Systems and Change Management. He is a member of Decision Sciences Institute (DSI), International Association for Computer Information Systems (IACIS), and International Chinese Information Systems Association (ICISA). He is VP Technology of ICISA.

Professor Chen holds a PhD degree from the University of Nebraska -Lincoln, a MBA degree from Northern Illinois University, a BS degree from Xi'an University of Technology. His website is web.stcloudstate.edu/qjchen, and he can be reached at jchen@stcloudstate.edu.
